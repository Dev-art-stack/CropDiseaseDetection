Although existing deep learning approaches report high accuracy for crop disease detection, most evaluations are restricted to the PlantVillage dataset captured under controlled laboratory conditions, limiting real-world applicability. Furthermore, prior works often employ computationally heavy models unsuitable for mobile deployment, lack uncertainty awareness and explainability, and do not provide end-to-end deployment pipelines. To address these gaps, this work proposes a domain-adapted, lightweight, and explainable CNN-based framework deployed as an on-device mobile application, enabling robust multi-crop disease detection in real-world scenarios.
